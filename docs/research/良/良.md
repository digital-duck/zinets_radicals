## Oracle Bone and Bronze Script Analysis

### Pictographic Interpretation of Oracle Forms (REVISED)

**Critical observation**: The oracle bone forms show a distinctive structure with a **square or opening in the middle**.

**Proposed interpretation**:
- **Central square/opening** = **SEED**
- **Downward extending lines** = **ROOTS** growing down into soil
- **Upward extending lines** = **STEM and LEAVES** growing up toward light
- **Overall form** = **GERMINATION IN PROGRESS** - the moment a seed becomes "good/viable"

**Material culture context - Seed Viability Testing**:

In Neolithic agricultural society, **testing seed viability** was literally life-or-death:
- Plant non-viable seeds → crop failure → starvation → societal collapse
- Must identify which seeds will germinate BEFORE committing them to planting
- Testing method: Germinate sample seeds, observe root and shoot emergence
- Seeds showing growth (良) = good, viable, life-sustaining, worthy of planting
- Seeds without growth = bad, dead, useless, must be discarded

**The parallel to human fertility - Evidence from 娘**:

The character **娘 (woman/mother) = 女 + 良** provides the key confirmatory insight:
- A "good woman" (良) in ancient context = woman with **fertility/capacity to bear children**
- Just as "good seed" (良) = seed with **capacity to germinate/grow**
- Both represent **GROWTH CAPACITY**, **LIFE-GENERATING ABILITY**
- Ancient survival depends equally on:
  - Botanical fertility (viable seeds → crops → food)
  - Human fertility (fertile women → children → labor → societal continuation)

**Universal principle**: **Growth = Good / No Growth = Bad**
- This applies to: seeds, crops, children, livestock, population, society
- Growth ensures survival and continuation
- Absence of growth means death and extinction

**Why 良 means both "germination" AND "happiness"**:
- Germinating seeds → successful planting → abundant harvest → food surplus → survival secured → **HAPPINESS**
- The dictionary records "happiness" (本义) because that's the **emotional consequence**
- But the **underlying material practice** is germination/growth observation
- "Happiness" is derivative; "germination" is primitive
