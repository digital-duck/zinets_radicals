### Working Hypothesis

**Proposed core semantic: "NARROW/FINE/CONSTRAINED"**

肙 may represent the concept of **enclosed/constrained flesh or form**, which materially grounds in:
- Narrow water channels (涓)
- Fine silk threads from constrained cocoons (绢)
- Delicate refined beauty (娟)
- Narrow bindings/straps (鞙)
- Constrained motion (蜎 wriggling, 睊 sideways glance)
- Narrow mental states (狷 narrow-minded, 悁 anxious constraint)
- Particular/selective action (涓 to select, 捐 to donate specifically)

This contrasts with broad, open, expansive concepts - instead emphasizing refinement through constraint.

## Seal Script Evidence

### 娟 (juān) - Beautiful/Graceful

**Seal script forms documented:**
- **Small seal (说文解字)**: S09365 - Shows 女 + 肙 structure clearly
- **Liushutong (六书通)**: L07031 - Confirms structural composition

**Character decomposition from Shuowen**:
- Compound character 娟 from 女 (female) + 肙 (phonetic small-worm 肙 yuán)
- Note: Traditional analysis treats 肙 as purely phonetic
- **Our reinterpretation**: 肙 contributes semantic meaning "delicate/fine/refined"

**Significance**:
- 娟 appears in seal script (late Zhou/Qin dynasty) but NOT in oracle bone or bronze
- This suggests the character developed during Zhou dynasty
- Original meaning explicitly stated as "beautiful and graceful" (本义: Meaning beautiful and graceful)
- Historical usage: Common in female given names, emphasizing refined/delicate beauty

**Semantic validation**:
- The combination 女 (woman) + 肙 (delicate/fine) → 娟 (delicate beauty) is semantically transparent
- This CONFIRMS that 肙 carries semantic weight, not just phonetic function
- Supports hypothesis: 肙 = "narrow/fine/constrained/delicate"
