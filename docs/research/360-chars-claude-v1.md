## Overlap Analysis

**Total Basic Characters**: 515
**Overlapping with Semantic Radicals**: 148 (28.7%)

These 148 overlapping characters are **priority characters** for Year 1 learning, as they provide both:
1. **Practical literacy** (high-frequency standalone usage)
2. **Structural foundation** (semantic radical building blocks)

### Key Insights

**High Overlap Categories**:
- **Nature (自然)**: Nearly all elemental characters (金木水火土, 天日月, 山川江河海) are semantic radicals
- **Human Body (身体)**: Core body part characters (心血骨肉, 目耳口鼻舌) serve as productive radicals
- **Animals (动物)**: Animal category characters (虫鱼鸟兽, 牛马羊犬) are fundamental radicals
- **Plants (植物)**: Plant-related characters (根芽枝叶, 草花树林) have high semantic productivity

**Learning Strategy**:
- **Year 1 Priority**: Focus on the 148 characters marked with ★
- **Year 2 Extension**: Add remaining ~216 semantic radicals not in basic character set
- **Total Mastery**: 360 radicals + 360 basic characters = comprehensive Chinese literacy foundation
