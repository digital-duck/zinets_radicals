## Chapter 3: The Discovery of Semantic Networks

Through systematic analysis, 文光 developed network coherence validation - testing character interpretations across entire families with scientific rigor:

**艮 (gěn) - The 95%+ Breakthrough:**
- 根 (root): Hidden underground = OUT OF VIEW ✓
- 银 (silver): Shiny metal = CATCHES THE EYE ✓
- 很 (very): Clearly marked = EASY TO SEE ✓
- 限 (limit): Boundary of sight = VISIBILITY LIMIT ✓

This 95%+ coherence proved that 艮 = visibility/sight, correcting Shuowen's 1,900-year-old interpretation of "stiff/hard" with scientific confidence.

## Chapter 4: The Agricultural Insight

During proof-reading the 140-page manuscript - gaining the balanced perspective that only physical paper can provide - 文光 discovered 垠 (boundary) = 土 + 艮. The visible edge of farmland furrows that farmers can walk on perfectly validated the 艮 = visibility interpretation.

## Chapter 5: The Mathematical Revolution - 数 = 女 + 米

Then came the breakthrough that would reshape understanding of mathematical history:

**数 (shù) - Number/Count = 女 (woman) + 米 (grain)**

This character composition revealed explosive truth: **Women invented mathematics** through agricultural bookkeeping! While men worked the fields (男 = 田 + 力), women managed the numerical accounting of harvest, storage, and trade.

This discovery provided:
- **Primary archaeological evidence** of women's foundational role in mathematics

- **Educational transformation** potential through historical connection
- **Cross-cultural research** methodology for investigating similar patterns globally
